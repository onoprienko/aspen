Today we would like to bring to your attention - Triada Tower. New project from Fundamental Architects. This is a case-study for a 222-meter sustainable skyscraper in Chicago, US. This mixed-used tower rises to become a prominent feature of the skyline and dictate a new sustainable approach in high-rise design and construction. We tried to work through every detail to catch the spirit of the building on the renderings.
